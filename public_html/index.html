About Me

My professional journey began with a passion for structuring complex and unorganized data, evolving into a deep specialization in big data analytics and intelligent decision-support systems.

In the early stages of my career, Microsoft Excel became my primary analytical platform—transformed from a simple spreadsheet tool into a robust environment for data modeling, visualization, and reporting. My advanced proficiency in PivotTables, Power Query, Power Pivot, and data modeling techniques enabled me to convert raw and fragmented datasets into dynamic and actionable insights.

One of my major achievements was the design and implementation of a Flat Data Warehouse, which centralized sales, performance, and financial data from multiple sources. This architecture, integrated with Excel and ERP systems, became the foundation for automated reporting and smart analytics within the organization.

Currently, I am focused on advancing my technical expertise in Python, Django, SQL, and Data Engineering, with a strong interest in building scalable and intelligent data pipelines for efficient data collection, transformation, and analysis.

My long-term vision is to develop my personal platform, XWork—an innovative ecosystem designed to help individuals manage their professional identity and career growth. XWork aims to integrate AI and data analytics to empower users in making smarter, data-driven career decisions.
